Your task is to clean a raw markdown text of a scanned mathematics textbook.

You can see a {raw_start}, {raw_end} lines of the raw markdown.

```markdown
# Chapter X
Preamble
Statement of Theorem A
Remarks
Theorem A Proof
More Remarks
```
## Task
Clean the raw text in the window below and add comment lines denoting the structural hierarchy.

## Cleaning Rules
- Fix: broken LaTeX (unclosed $, split expressions), broken markdown, page numbers, headers/footers, watermarks, redundant blank lines, broken paragraph joins.
- Preserve: all math content and notation, the author's voice, math environments, lists, theory blocks.
- Determine which headers (#, ##, ...) are genuine, some can be OCR mistakes. Put genuine headings into the structural comment tree.
- Theorems should be their own node. The theorem proof can be their own node.
